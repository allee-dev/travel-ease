Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit Northern Pakistan?

The best time to visit is from April to October, with peak season being May to September. Weather is most favorable during these months for trekking and sightseeing.

Do I need special permits for certain areas?

Yes, some areas require special permits. We handle all permit applications and documentation as part of our service.

What's included in the trip packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, meals, transportation, guide services, and permits. Specific inclusions vary by package.

Can I customize a package to my preferences?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries tailored to your interests, budget, and travel style.

What safety measures do you have in place?

We have comprehensive safety protocols including trained guides, emergency communication systems, first aid kits, and 24/7 support.

What should I pack for the trip?

We provide a detailed packing list based on your specific itinerary, season, and activities. Essential items include warm clothing, sturdy hiking boots, and sun protection.
